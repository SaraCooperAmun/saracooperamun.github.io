## About me

I am a robotics and biomedical engineer specialising in **social robotics for healthcare and assistive applications**. My work focuses on deploying social robots in real-world scenarios, combining **robotics software development, research, and project coordination** across industry and academia in Spain, Japan, and the UK.

---

## Education

**Master in Robotics (with Distinction)**  
Heriot-Watt University, Edinburgh, UK — *2018–2019*  
- Robotics Operating System (ROS), biologically inspired computation, robot kinematics  
- MSc thesis on **human–robot object handover with TIAGo**, in collaboration with psychologists from the University of Stirling  
- EEG-based cognitive load analysis (user study with 16 participants)

**Bachelor in Biomedical Engineering**  
Mondragon Unibertsitatea, Mondragon, Spain — *2014–2018*  
- Project-based learning: infant incubator, ECG acquisition system, hip implant design  
- Final-year project at **Middlesex University (London)** using the **Pepper robot** for cognitive stimulation  
- User study with 10 older adults
